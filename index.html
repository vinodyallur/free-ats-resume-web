Tips for the best result

  • After downloading, open in Word or Google Docs, proofread, then export to PDF.
  • Mirror keywords from the job description in your Skills and Experience sections.
  • Keep it to one page — trim the oldest or least-relevant bullets if it overflows.
  • Scanned/image-only PDFs have no selectable text and can't be parsed (no OCR).
